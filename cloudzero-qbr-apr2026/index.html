AI Strategy — DevRev + Claude, Better Together

Starting from a position of strength: CloudZero is already an AI-forward company — Claude Enterprise is in active use, and your team is managing DevRev through MCP. This isn't a conversation about AI adoption. It's a conversation about where DevRev's AI layer adds the most value given what you already have.

Where DevRev AI is differentiated vs. Claude

  • ★AI ticket deflection (25–40% target). Claude doesn't sit in front of your customer portal. Your configured AI agent does — and once the KB is populated, it deflects inbound before a ticket is ever created. Claude can't do that.
  • ★AI-driven ticket routing. Auto-assign by severity, product area, and on-call schedule — directly inside your ticket workflow. Tom's prioritization formula can become a live routing rule, not a Python script run manually.
  • →Structured workflows, not just answers. Claude answers questions. DevRev's AI layer executes workflows — stage transitions, assignments, CSAT triggers, escalations. Different job, same AI-forward team.
  • →Confluence connector. Surface engineering docs in ticket context — agents (human and AI) get the right answer without leaving DevRev.
